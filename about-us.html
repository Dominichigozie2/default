ABOUT AfriqVax

Your Right Path To Smart health Decisions

AfriqVax was incorporated in 2024 in response to the African Union ambitious goal, to have manufacturers based on the continent develop, produce, and supply more than 60% of the vaccine doses required on the continent by 2040. Also, Nigeria being the most populous country in Africa exploring local manufacture of some of the routine vaccines as a sustainable exit strategy from Global Alliance for Vaccination and Immunization (GAVI) support.
