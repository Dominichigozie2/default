ENVIRONMENT

Elevate Your Health Naturally

At AfriqVax, we recognize that safeguarding human health goes hand in hand with protecting our planet. As a leader in vaccine development, we are dedicated to minimizing our environmental impact and promoting sustainability across all aspects of our operations. We will uphold our environmental responsibility by sustainable manufacturing practices, waste management and recycling, energy and water conservation, community engagement and education, transparency and accountability etc.

ETHICAL STANDARDS

Embrace Vallues That Matters

All our dealings with third parties are carried out in compliance with all relevant laws and regulations and with the highest standards of integrity required. We declare that corrupt practices are not acceptable in Afriqvax business dealings both in the private and government sectors, Afriqvax’s dealings with governments and government officials are subject to specific anticorruption laws which carry very significant penalties, including but not limited to the UK Anti-Terrorism Crime and Security Act 2001, the US Foreign Corrupt Practices Act and the US Sarbanes-Oxley Act.

CORPORATE CITIZENSHIP

Leading with Purpose and Responsibility

Corporate responsibility is central to everything we do. We recognize that doing business in a responsible manner is integral to ensuring our future license to operate. We aim to integrate our CSR considerations into all our business decisions, to comply with all applicable legislation, regulations and codes of practice and to review the annual report and to continually strive to improve our CSR performance

OUR COREVALUES

Dedicated to Uncompromising Quality

In the heart of everything we do, we encourage quality, from the product development, to the manufacture, supply and sale.
